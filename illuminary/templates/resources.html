Resources

Immediate Help:

In life-threatening situations, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

If you are suicidal or in emotional distress:
  • Use the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline by calling or texting 988 or chatting online. This provides 24-hour, confidential support.
  • In case of a disaster, reach out to the Disaster Distress Helpline by calling or texting 1-800-985-59901.

If you see concerning social media messages, you can use our product to determine the state of the user, and then contact the company’s safety team. Social media companies have safety teams that can connect the person with help.

Improving Mental Health Throughout the Day:

Get Restful Sleep: Prioritize quality sleep—it’s essential for mental health.

Cut Back on Social Media: Limit screen time to reduce stress.

Strengthen Relationships: Connect with friends and loved ones.

Stay Active: Physical activity positively impacts mental well-being.

Savor Nutrient-Rich Foods: A balanced diet supports energy and focus.

Know When to Take It Easy: Practice self-compassion and rest when needed.

Get Some Sunshine: Sunlight boosts mood and vitamin D levels.
